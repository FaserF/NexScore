# 🔊 Audio & Speech Guide

NexScore provides immersive feedback through Sound Effects (SFX) and Text-To-Speech (TTS) to enhance your gameplay experience.

## 🎵 Sound Effects (SFX)

Sounds are triggered by various actions in the app:
- **Swipe Actions**: In party games like SipDeck, swiping cards triggers a subtle "beep".
- **Interactions**: Tapping buttons or crossing fields in Qwixx provides haptic-like click sounds.
- **Victory**: Determination of a winner triggers a celebratory fanfare.

### 🔇 Muting Sounds
You can disable all sound effects in the **Settings** menu:
1. Navigate to **Settings** (⚙️ icon on Home).
2. Toggle the **"Sound Effects"** switch.

## 🗣️ Text-To-Speech (TTS)

For party games (SipDeck, BuzzTap) or WayQuest, NexScore can read out the prompts for you. This is perfect for loud environments or when your hands are full (e.g., during a drinking game).

### 🎙️ Using TTS
1. Open a game that supports TTS (e.g., SipDeck).
2. Look for the **Speaker (🔊)** icon in the AppBar or card area.
3. Once enabled, the current card text will be read aloud automatically whenever you swipe or reveal a new prompt.

### ⚙️ Language Support
TTS will automatically use the language currently set in your NexScore settings (English or German).

---
*Note: TTS quality and availability depend on your device's built-in speech engine.*
