# Volleyball Score Tracker - User Guide 🏐

A professional-grade digital scoreboard for tracking official volleyball matches, following the 2026 German Volleyball League (Dvv) regulations.

## 🏆 Match Modes

### 1. Indoor Volleyball
- **Sets to Win**: Best of 5 (3 sets).
- **Points per Set**: 25 points.
- **Deciding Set**: 15 points (5th set).
- **Timeouts**: 2 per set.

### 2. Beach Volleyball
- **Sets to Win**: Best of 3 (2 sets).
- **Points per Set**: 21 points.
- **Deciding Set**: 15 points (3rd set).
- **Side Switch**: Automatically reminds you to switch sides every 7 points (cumulative score).
- **Timeouts**: 1 per set.

## 🕹 Features

### 1. Scoring Logic
- **Add Point**: Tap the large score area for a team.
- **Remove Point**: Long-press the score area (for accidental entries).
- **Server Tracking**: The ball icon 🏐 indicates which team is currently serving. The server automatically switches when the receiving team wins a rally.

### 2. Match Reports (Official PDF)
At the end of a match, you can generate a professional PDF report.
- **Signatures**: Capture digital signatures from both team captains and the referee directly on the device.
- **Summary**: The PDF includes set-by-set scores and the final winner.
- **Sharing**: Use the system share sheet to send the report via WhatsApp, Email, or AirDrop.

### 3. Referee Signals
Access the **Rule** icon in the AppBar to see a visual guide of official referee hand signals (e.g., Ball In, Ball Out, Net Touch, Four Hits).

## 📊 Multiplayer
Standard NexScore multiplayer synchronization is supported. The host manages the scoreboard, and the score updates in real-time on all spectator/player devices.
