# 📸 Share-To-Story Guide

NexScore allows you to share your game victories and party challenges directly to social media stories (Instagram, WhatsApp, etc.).

## 🏆 Sharing Game Results

After finishing a game of **Kniffel**, **Wizard**, **Schafkopf**, or **Qwixx**, you can celebrate your win:

1.  Press the **Emoji Events (🏆)** button in the AppBar.
2.  In the celebration overlay, click **"Share Results"**.
3.  A premium-designed results card will be generated.
4.  Choose your target app (e.g., Instagram Story) from the share sheet.

The shared image includes:
-   The winner's name and emoji.
-   The game name.
-   A ranked list of the top players.
-   The NexScore branding.

## 🃏 Sharing Party Prompts

In **SipDeck** and **BuzzTap**, you can share individual cards or lightning-fast dares:

1.  While a card is displayed, locate the **Share** icon next to the "Skip/Next" buttons.
2.  Click the share icon to capture the specific prompt.
3.  The shared card will automatically use the category color and an appropriate emoji.

## 🛠 Technical Notes

- **Capture Quality**: NexScore captures widgets at a 3.0 pixel ratio to ensure they look sharp on all social platforms.
- **Privacy**: Sharing images are stored temporarily on your device and are cleared after the share is complete.
