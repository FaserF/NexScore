# Getting Started with NexScore 🏁

NexScore is designed to be intuitive and fast. This guide will help you set up and start tracking your games in minutes.

## Installation 📱

NexScore is available on multiple platforms:

- **Web**: Access the app directly at [https://faserf.github.io/NexScore/](https://faserf.github.io/NexScore/)
- **Mobile**: Coming soon to the App Store and Google Play Store.
- **Desktop**: Binary releases are available on our [GitHub Releases](https://github.com/FaserF/NexScore/releases) page for Windows, macOS, and Linux.

## First Steps 🚶‍♂️

### Adding Players

To start tracking scores, you first need to add your players:

1. Navigate to the **Players** tab.
2. Tap the **+** (Add) button.
3. Enter the player's name and choose a color.
4. Tap **Add**.

### Starting a Game

1. Navigate to the **Games** tab.
2. Choose a game type (e.g., Wizard, Qwixx, or Generic).
3. Select the players participating in this session.
4. Tap **Start Game**.

### Tracking Scores

Each game has a custom interface tailored to its rules:

- **Wizard**: Track bids and tricks won for each round. NexScore calculates the points automatically!
- **Qwixx**: Tap the numbers as you cross them off your physical card.
- **Generic**: Simply enter the points for each player at the end of the game or after each round.

## Synchronization 🔄

To keep your data safe across devices, we recommend signing in:

1. Go to the **Account** tab.
2. Sign in with **Google** (best for mobile/web) or **GitHub** (great for power users).
3. Once signed in, your sessions and players will sync automatically.

!!! tip "GitHub Power Users"
    If you sign in with GitHub, you can use the **Gist Backup** feature to save your data as a private Gist in your own account. This gives you full control and ownership of your gaming history!
